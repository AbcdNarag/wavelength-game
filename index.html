How to Play
  • 1A random spectrum pair appears each round. You can shuffle or edit it anytime.
  • 2The Psychic secretly sees where the target sits on the dial, then gives one clue out loud.
  • 3The Team debates and drags the needle to their best guess, then locks in.
  • 4Closer = more points. Play as many rounds as you like!
